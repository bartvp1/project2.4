Welcome to meetup!



Here you can find new friends based on your hobbies.



Follow these steps to start making friends:

  1. Navigate to "My hobbies"

  2. Add hobbies to your profile

  3. Navigate to "Matches"

  4. There you will see the contact-information of users, who share hobbies with you



Have fun meeting up with new people!
